Space Janitor

a javascript adventure by Ian Goodrich and Nico Daunt

Good morning . You've been sleeping for 127 years. Actually, you've been in a state of cyro-stasis, frozen alongside hundreds of passengers on the SS Miskatonic. You are currently in transit, on a journey to New Earth, scheduled for an arrival in 2,011 years. Unfortunately, your cyro-crypt has malfunctioned, which is why you'll notice that all other passengers aboard are still in hibernation. Lucky for you, the SS Miskatonic is in need of a handyman. The original engineers overlooked the necessity of active personnel onboard the fleet, and after a century worth of travel, the ship has picked up some unintended stowaways. Never to fear, armed with your recently thawed fists, and a cache of hidden weapons, you should be able to take care of these unknown creatures, while maintaining the safety of your fellow frozen humans.
